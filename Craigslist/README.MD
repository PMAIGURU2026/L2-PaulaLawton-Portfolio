# 🏘️ Craigslist Clone: Transparent Advertising Model
### Pursuit AI Fellowship — Level 2 | Week 3 Pair Project
### Business Model Assignment: Advertisement

## What It Does
A Craigslist clone that improves on the original by making
the advertising business model visible and transparent to
everyday users. The platform clearly distinguishes paid
listings from free ones — surfacing the revenue model
directly in the user experience.

## Why We Built It
Craigslist generates ~$302M/year from paid listings but
users rarely understand what they're paying for or why.
Our clone makes that transparent — when a user selects
Jobs or Housing, the UI dynamically shows pricing and
surfaces a payment step, making the business model
tangible and honest.

## The Key Improvement 💡
The original Craigslist hides its monetization. Our clone
surfaces it with:
- Dynamic pricing display when paid categories are selected
- "PAID LISTING" badge with distinct visual styling
- Clear separation of paid vs. free content throughout
- Transparent checkout flow for job and housing posts

## Revenue Model (Craigslist-Inspired)
- Job listings: $25 per post
- Housing listings: $5 per post
- Everything else: Free
- Source: AIM Group 2024 — Jobs (35.6%), Services (31%),
  Auto/Dealer (20.3%) of Craigslist revenue
