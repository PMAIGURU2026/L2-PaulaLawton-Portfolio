# 💰 Savings Depot
### Pursuit AI Fellowship — Level 2

## What It Does
A freemium mobile app that consolidates all of a user's 
rewards cards, loyalty memberships, and club memberships 
in one place. Users upload barcodes, QR codes, member IDs, 
and even insurance cards — accessible instantly at checkout.

## Why We Built It
People lose time and miss savings because their rewards cards 
are scattered across wallets, apps, and emails. 
Savings Depot solves that with one simple hub.

## Frontend Stack
- Framework: React Native (mobile)
- UI: Custom components — Turquoise + Gold brand palette
- Animations: Floating success animation on card upload
- Auth: User login with encrypted credential storage
- Deploy: Apple App Store + Google Play (Freemium model)

## Backend Stack
- Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL (user accounts, card storage)
- Encryption: Industry-standard data encryption
- Storage: Barcode/QR image storage
- Ads: Google AdMob (freemium revenue model)
